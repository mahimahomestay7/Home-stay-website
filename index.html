Our Story

Peace, Comfort & Genuine Hospitality

Mahima Homestay is a family-run accommodation in the heart of Bylukuppa — one of Karnataka's most tranquil destinations, known for its Tibetan monasteries and lush greenery.

We offer clean, comfortable rooms and genuine homely hospitality. Whether you're visiting the Golden Temple, exploring the local culture, or simply resting after a long journey — we make sure you feel right at home.

Our location near Salumarada Thimmakka Park makes us easily accessible and a perfect base for exploring everything the region has to offer.
